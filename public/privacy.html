Privacy questions, access requests, and complaints can be sent to [privacy@liaisonreply.com](mailto:privacy@liaisonreply.com). **Operating model:** Liaison Reply drafts AI-generated reply suggestions for your review. You retain complete editorial control and decide what to edit, copy, or send. --- ## 1. Legal Bases for Processing We process personal data based on: explicit consent, contractual necessity, legal obligation, or legitimate interests. --- ## 2. Data We Collect ### Automatically Collected - IP address and approximate geolocation - Device type, operating system, browser details - Page views, feature usage, interaction telemetry - Security/diagnostics logs ### User-Submitted - Email address and auth credentials - Payment/subscription metadata - Support communications - User preferences and settings - Messages, conversations, and contextual input for AI generation --- ## 3. How We Use Data - Service operation and account management - Payment processing and billing - AI reply draft generation at user request - Service security, fraud prevention, abuse detection - Product analytics and improvement - Essential service communications --- ## 4. AI Model Training Guarantee — Section of Material Importance **Core guarantee:** Your user-submitted messages, conversation history, and contextual input are **strictly isolated and NEVER used to train, fine-tune, or improve any foundational AI model.** ### 4.1 Google Gemini API Processing Liaison Reply uses Google's Gemini API. Under Google's public API terms: - Google may retain prompts/outputs for **up to 55 days** for abuse monitoring - **Google does NOT use your API data to train foundational models** without obtaining separate opt-in consent - Our integration is configured with model training disabled; **no user data is repurposed for training** We maintain technical safeguards to prevent data repurposing and audit third-party provider agreements to confirm this restriction. --- ## 5. Special Category Data (GDPR Article 9) We may process message content revealing special category data (health, political opinions, religious beliefs, racial/ethnic origin, sexual orientation) **only with your explicit affirmative consent** obtained during registration. You may revoke consent at any time in Settings → Privacy & Data. Non-consent blocks AI generation features but allows basic account use. Special category data processed via Gemini API is retained for a maximum of **55 days** for abuse monitoring, then automatically deleted. --- ## 6. We provide two deletion pathways: #### **Method A: In-App Instant Deletion** 1. Navigate to **Settings → Account → Delete Account** 2. Confirm your identity via your logged-in session 3. Your request is processed immediately **What is deleted instantly:** - All message threads, conversation history, and AI-generated drafts - Your user profile, preferences, and settings - Auth session and access tokens **What purges automatically within 72 hours:** - Backups containing your data are marked for immediate expiration - Third-party service logs (Gemini API prompts/outputs, Stripe billing records) are requested for deletion via automated forwarding - Analytics and diagnostic data attributable to your account is anonymized #### **Method B: Email-Initiated Deletion** 1. Email [privacy@liaisonreply.com](mailto:privacy@liaisonreply.com) with subject line **"ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST"** 2. Include the email address associated with your account 3. We will verify your identity and process the request within 24 hours All deletions are final and irreversible. We will confirm completion via email. ### 6.3 Granular Data Deletion and Portability You may delete individual conversation threads at any time from the thread view.
