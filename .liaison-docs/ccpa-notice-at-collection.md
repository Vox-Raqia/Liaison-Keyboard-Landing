## Notice at Collection for California Residents

This notice is provided to California residents in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

### Categories of Personal Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of personal information:
- **Identifiers**: Name, email address, IP address, user ID
- **Personal information**: Message content you submit for AI reply generation, user-provided context
- **Internet activity information**: Browser history, interaction with our site and app
- **Inferences**: Preferences, interests, and other characteristics derived from your usage data

### Sources of Personal Information
We collect personal information from:
- Information you provide directly (account creation, message submission, support requests)
- Automatic collection through cookies and similar technologies
- Third-party service providers (analytics, payment processing, etc.)

### Purposes for Collection and Use
We use your personal information for:
- Providing and maintaining our services
- Generating AI-powered reply suggestions
- Processing payments and managing subscriptions
- Improving our products through analytics and feedback
- Ensuring security and preventing fraud
- Communicating with you about service updates and support
- Complying with legal obligations

### Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your personal information with:
- Service providers necessary to operate our service (Stripe, Supabase, PostHog, etc.)
- Payment processors for billing transactions
- Analytics providers to understand usage patterns
- AI model providers to generate reply suggestions
- Legal authorities when required by law

### Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- **Know** what personal information we collect, use, and share
- **Delete** personal information we have collected from you
- **Opt-out** of the sale or sharing of personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Limit** use of sensitive personal information
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising your privacy rights

### How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise your CCPA/CPRA rights, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@liaisonreply.com
- Subject line: "CCPA Request"

We will verify your identity and respond to your request within 45 days, as required by law.

### Do Not Sell My Personal Information
Liaison Reply does not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA. However, we may share information with service providers for business purposes. If you wish to opt-out of such sharing, please submit a request via the contact information above.

### Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

### Changes to This Notice
We may update this notice from time to time. The version date at the top indicates the last revision.
