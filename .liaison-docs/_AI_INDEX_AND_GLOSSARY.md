### Glossary
- **Safe / Warm / Direct**: The three reply options generated by Liaison Reply. Safe = reduce heat and stay clear. Warm = keep the door open and move forward. Direct = hold the line without over-explaining. These are the canonical labels — do not use Natural / Keep it going / Short anywhere on the marketing site or in documentation.
- **Hesitant Texter**: Persona for early dating, mixed signals, and reconnecting situations.
- **Conflict Avoider**: Persona for family friction, friend fallout, tense ex, and coworker conflict.
- **Careful Professional**: Persona for negotiating, pushing back on a boss, and careful client wording.
- **DPIA**: Data Protection Impact Assessment
- **GDPR**: General Data Protection Regulation
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act
- **CPRA**: California Privacy Rights Act
- **SLA**: Service Level Agreement
- **DPO**: Data Protection Officer
- **SSOT**: Single Source of Truth
