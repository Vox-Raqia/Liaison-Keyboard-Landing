# CCPA Notice at Collection

**Last updated:** 2026-04-27

This notice applies to California residents at or before the point of data
collection.

## Categories of personal information

| Category | Examples | Business purpose | Shared with |
|---|---|---|---|
| Identifiers | Email address, account ID, IP address, Stripe customer ID | Account creation, security, billing, support | Supabase, Stripe, Resend |
| Internet or network activity | Device/browser data, app events, page views, diagnostics | Security, analytics, product improvement | PostHog, Google Analytics |
| Commercial information | Subscription status, checkout session ID, billing metadata | Payments, invoicing, entitlement management | Stripe |
| User-generated content | Messages, optional context, generated replies | Provide the drafting product you requested | Google Gemini, Supabase |
| Support records | Emails and support tickets | Customer support and incident response | Resend, support tooling |

## Retention

Retention periods are described in the main privacy policy and may vary by
record type, legal obligation, and deletion request status.

## Sensitive personal information

Message content may contain sensitive personal information or special-category
data. Liaison Reply processes that content only to provide the drafting service
you requested and for related security/abuse controls.

## Sale or sharing

Liaison Reply does not sell personal information for money. Optional analytics
and advertising-related technologies should remain consent-gated. If California
law treats any analytics transfer as "sharing," users can opt out through the
cookie settings and by contacting privacy@liaisonreply.com.

## Your choices

- Use cookie settings to deny optional analytics cookies.
- Contact privacy@liaisonreply.com to exercise access, deletion, correction, or
  opt-out rights.
- Use account settings to manage or delete in-product data where supported.

## Do Not Sell or Share

Requests can be sent to:

- `privacy@liaisonreply.com`
- Subject line: `California Privacy Request`
