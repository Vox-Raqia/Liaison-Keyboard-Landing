# Stripe Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**SLA URL:** https://stripe.com/en-ca/legal/ssa  
**Service:** Payment Processing & Billing  
**Effective Date:** TBD  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25 (sourced from public SSA)

## Service Description

Stripe provides:
- Payment acceptance (credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Subscription management and recurring billing
- Webhook event delivery
- Dashboard and reporting tools
- Fraud prevention (Radar)

## Service Commitments

### Uptime/Availability
- **Public SSA Position:** The main Services Agreement does not explicitly state a universal uptime percentage.
- Service-specific terms or enterprise agreements may define uptime commitments per payment product.
- For definitive SLA terms, refer to your contract or Stripe's service-specific documentation.

### Performance
- No explicit performance metrics (response time, processing time) stated in the main SSA.
- Implicit obligation to provide services with "commercially reasonable" care and skill (per Section 8.2 Disclaimers).

### Support
- Stripe provides basic business and technical support via email and chat through standard channels.
- No guaranteed initial response times are specified in the main SSA.
- Optional paid support plans may offer priority response times.
- **Contact:** https://support.stripe.com/
- **Emergency Phone:** +1 (855) 473-4273 (24/7)

## Remedies

### Service Credits
- Not explicitly detailed in the public SSA document.
- Service credit terms, if any, are defined in service-specific terms or enterprise agreements.
- Refer to your order form or contract for specific credit policies.

### Claim Procedure
If credits are applicable under your agreement:
1. Submit claim within 30 days of month-end.
2. Contact Stripe support via https://support.stripe.com/contact.

## Exclusions

SLA limitations (as typical and per SSA Section 8 and service terms):
- Customer equipment/software/third-party services failures
- Scheduled maintenance (with notice, typically ≥ 72 hours)
- Force majeure events
- Customer violation of terms or rate limiting
- Issues outside Stripe's reasonable control

## Data Protection & Security

- Data Processing Agreement (DPA): https://stripe.com/en-ca/legal/dpa
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) available for international transfers.
- Security measures: encryption in transit, PCI DSS compliance, access controls.
- Breach notification: Customer must notify Stripe immediately of unauthorized personal data acquisition.
