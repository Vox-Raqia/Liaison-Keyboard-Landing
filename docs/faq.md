# Liaison Reply FAQ

## General Questions

### What exactly is Liaison Reply?
Liaison Reply is an app that helps you answer difficult texts. You paste the message, review three reply options (Natural, Keep it going, Short), edit the one that fits, and send it yourself.

### Does anything auto-send?
No. Liaison Reply never sends on your behalf. You review, edit, copy, and decide what goes back into the conversation.

### Why the name Liaison Reply?
The product helps you slow down, read the moment clearly, and choose a stronger response before anything gets sent. The name reflects the real job: helping you reply with more clarity, judgment, and control in high-stakes conversations.

### Who is Liaison Reply best for?
People who need help answering loaded messages at work, in dating, or with family without freezing, spiraling, or firing off something they regret.

## Features & Usage

### What do I get with the free tier?
You get 15 free generations so you can test the full workflow before paying. That is enough to see whether the product helps you think and reply more clearly.

### What changes when I upgrade to Pro?
Pro gives you more room to keep going when the conversation gets difficult, plus deeper conversation memory, premium personas (The Diplomat, The Charmer, The Closer, The Cool Head), and priority support for higher-stakes situations.

### How does the three-reply system work?
The engine analyzes the sender's tone and social dynamics to generate three distinct, humanized responses optimized for specific communication outcomes:
- Natural: Standard response that maintains your authentic voice
- Keep it going: Proactive response to move the conversation forward  
- Short: Concise response to hold boundaries or de-escalate

### What is "Controlled Execution"?
Users have two distinct options: Copy or Edit & Copy. This guarantees that only the final, sent text is saved to the app's database, ensuring perfect context accuracy for future replies.

### What is "Isolated Yet Linkable Memory"?
By default, each conversation acts as a strictly isolated memory container to prevent context contamination. However, users have the ability to link and unlink distinct threads together to understand complex, multi-conversational dynamics across different people or platforms.

## Privacy & Data

### What happens to my messages and data?
Right now, Liaison Reply runs on the web. Nothing auto-sends, and only the content you choose to save stays in your account. Each conversation is isolated by default to prevent context contamination.

### Does Liaison Reply share my data with third parties?
No. We do not sell or share your personal data or conversation content with third parties for marketing purposes.

### How does cookie consent work?
Full Memory (unlimited conversation history) requires user-approved Cookie Consent. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie settings page.

## Technical Support

### I'm not getting reply generations. What should I do?
1. Ensure you have pasted a message and added any relevant context
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try refreshing the page
4. If the problem persists, contact support at support@liaisonreply.com

### Why am I getting blank or empty replies?
Blank generations usually indicate a webhook or API issue. Try:
1. Check that your message has content (not empty)
2. Verify you have generation credits (free tier limit: 15)
3. Refresh and retry
4. If blank persists, contact support with your user ID and timestamp

### Why didn't I get Pro features after payment?
This is a webhook sync issue. Your payment may have gone through but Pro access hasn't activated yet. Please:
1. Check your Stripe confirmation email
2. Contact support with your transaction ID
3. We'll manually verify and grant access within 4 hours

### How do I link conversation threads?
In the app, you can link threads by selecting the "Link Threads" option in the thread menu. This allows the engine to understand context across different conversations.

### How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual threads or conversations from the app interface via thread settings. For complete account deletion (GDPR/CCPA), contact support at support@liaisonreply.com. Account deletion is processed within 30 days.

### Why can't I edit my thread?
Threads become read-only 24 hours after your last activity to maintain context integrity. You can still view but not edit past messages in closed threads.

### Why did my session expire?
Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity for security. Simply log back in to start a new session. Your saved threads remain in your account.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/28E7sL3uUgOp35GeVSbfO00

### Why can't I access my account?
Common causes:
- Password reset needed (use "Forgot Password" on login)
- Account not yet activated (check confirmation email)
- Session expired (log in again)
If none of these work, contact support with your registered email.

### How do I report a bug or technical issue?
Email support@liaisonreply.com with:
1. Steps to reproduce
2. Browser and device details (Chrome/Safari, iOS/Android)
3. Screenshots or screen recording
4. Timestamp and timezone
5. Your user ID if logged in

We prioritize bugs that break core functionality (blank generations, crashes, data loss).

### What happens to my data when I delete the app?
Deleting the app does not delete your account data. To delete your data, either use the in-app delete option or email privacy@liaisonreply.com with a deletion request. Data is permanently removed within 30 days.

### Why do I need cookies for Full Memory?
Full Memory (unlimited conversation history) requires storing conversation context locally via cookies. You can enable this in cookie settings. Without cookie consent, only ephemeral memory is available.

### How do I enable/disable cookie consent?
Use the cookie settings page at /cookies.html or the cookie banner on first visit. You can update preferences at any time. Full Memory requires continuity cookies to be granted.

### What browsers are supported?
Liaison Reply works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile browsers are supported via the web app (no native app required).
