Terms

Terms for using Liaison Keyboard.

Last updated: March 19, 2026. These terms reflect the current standalone product experience, not the future roadmap for native keyboard extensions.

1. AI-generated assistance

Liaison Keyboard provides AI-generated reply suggestions. You are responsible for reviewing and deciding whether to use any output. Generated content should be treated as assistance, not guaranteed advice or factual verification.

2. Plans and billing

The current product offers a free tier and one Pro plan with monthly and yearly billing. The free tier uses a faster base AI experience. Pro adds premium AI, full conversation memory, and access to Pro personas. Current published pricing is $7.99 per month or $70.99 per year. Stripe manages checkout, invoices, payment methods, and subscription updates.

3. Cancellation and renewals

Pro subscriptions renew according to the interval you choose until canceled. You can manage or cancel a subscription through the Stripe customer portal made available from the product settings flow and support page.

4. Contact

Questions about billing, terms, or support can be sent to support@liaisonkeyboard.com.
