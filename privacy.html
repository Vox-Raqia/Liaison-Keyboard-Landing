Privacy

How Liaison Keyboard handles user data.

Last updated: March 22, 2026. This summary reflects the currently shipped product surfaces, including authentication, billing, generated replies, and saved conversation history.

1. What we process

Liaison Keyboard processes account details needed for authentication, subscription status, billing support, and product operations. When you use the product, the message context you submit is processed so the service can generate reply suggestions for the selected plan and persona experience attached to your account.

2. Saved conversation data

The current product includes saved threads and conversation history for signed-in users. That means the service is not a zero-retention system. We store the data needed to support chat history, plan entitlements, and product operations, and we limit access to the authenticated user scope used by the app.

3. Billing and infrastructure

Subscription checkout and billing management are handled through Stripe. Authentication, product data, and server-side functions run through our app infrastructure, including user-scoped access controls for account data. Depending on your plan, the service may route requests through different AI model tiers to deliver either the free experience or the Pro experience.

4. Privacy requests and support

If you need help with privacy, billing, or account data, email support@liaisonkeyboard.com. We use support conversations only to address the request you submit and operate the related support workflow.
