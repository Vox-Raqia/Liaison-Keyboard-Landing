Terms Support

1. No Data Persistence

Liaison Keyboard processes your submitted message context in volatile memory for generation only. Session context is deleted after generation unless you explicitly enable Pro history features.

2. Data Sovereignty and Infrastructure Security

Our cloud stack operates across AWS and Supabase Tier-IV aligned facilities with encryption in transit, encryption at rest, and strict Row-Level Security (RLS) enforcement for user-scoped records.

3. CCPA and GDPR 2026 Compliance

You may request access, correction, portability, or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@liaisonkeyboard.com. Verified deletion requests are processed in accordance with CCPA and GDPR 2026 obligations.

4. Contact
