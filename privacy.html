Privacy

How Liaison Keyboard handles user data.

Last updated: March 30, 2026. This policy covers both liaisonkeyboard.com and app.liaisonkeyboard.com.

What we collect and process

Liaison Keyboard collects account details (email, authentication credentials), billing metadata, submitted message context entered during generation sessions, and product telemetry necessary to operate and improve the service. Generated replies are assistive output that you review and copy yourself — nothing is sent on your behalf.

Analytics and usage tracking

This site and the connected app use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID G-FMVPQNPPDD) to collect aggregated page-view data, session duration, referral sources, and device-type information. This data is processed by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Google Analytics sets its own cookies (prefixed _ga) scoped to the current domain.

You can opt out of Google Analytics measurement across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies and local storage

Liaison Keyboard uses a small number of first-party cookies and localStorage entries to operate the site. The following keys are in use:

  • liaison_cookie_consent — stores your cookie preference (necessary-only or all). Persisted in both localStorage and as a cookie on .liaisonkeyboard.com for up to 180 days. Set immediately on first consent interaction.
  • lk_deeplink — stores attribution parameters (utm_source, ref_id, scenario_id, prefill) so they survive the handoff from the marketing site into the app. Stored in localStorage and as a cookie on .liaisonkeyboard.com for up to 30 days. Only written if you accept optional continuity cookies.
  • liaison_auth_hint — a lightweight flag that lets the marketing site detect whether a session is active so the navigation buttons can reflect your current state (e.g., "Back to Dashboard" instead of "Start Free"). Stored in localStorage and as a cookie on .liaisonkeyboard.com for up to 14 days. Only written if you accept optional continuity cookies.

All optional cookies are gated behind your explicit choice in the cookie banner or the cookie settings page. Selecting "Necessary only" prevents all continuity cookies from being written and clears any that were previously stored.

Attribution and referral tracking

When you arrive on liaisonkeyboard.com with URL parameters such as utm_source, ref_id, or scenario_id, Liaison Keyboard may store those values locally so they can be passed into the app during the same visit or a return visit. This attribution data is used only for continuity and internal analytics — it is never sold or shared with advertising platforms outside of the Google Analytics integration described above.

Full Memory is opt-in

Full Memory is a privacy-first opt-in feature available on Pro plans. When enabled, it lets Liaison Keyboard retain conversational context inside isolated memory containers so you can maintain continuity across threads. Full Memory is not active by default and requires explicit consent inside the app.

Thread isolation

Memory containers are designed to prevent one user's conversation context from leaking into another. Thread linking is treated as a user-directed action rather than a default behavior.

Billing and payments

Payment processing, subscription management, invoice delivery, and the customer billing portal are handled by Stripe. Liaison Keyboard does not store raw payment card data. Stripe's handling of payment information is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Authentication and data storage

Account authentication and user-scoped data storage for the app experience are handled through a managed backend service. User-submitted content is stored in isolated, user-scoped containers and is not used to train or improve AI models without explicit opt-in.

Interactive site features

The FAQ accordion and the hero example switcher (Work / Dating / Family) on the marketing site are fully client-side features. They do not transmit data to any server and do not set cookies or modify browser storage.

Data retention

Attribution cookies and session hints expire on the schedules noted in the Cookies section above. Google Analytics data is retained according to the retention setting configured in the associated Google Analytics property. Account and message data in the app is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support@liaisonkeyboard.com.

Your choices

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time from the cookie settings page. You can clear all site storage at any time through your browser settings. You can opt out of Google Analytics as described in the Analytics section above. You can disable Full Memory inside the app at any time. To request account deletion or a copy of your data, contact support@liaisonkeyboard.com.

Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns about this policy can be sent to support@liaisonkeyboard.com.
