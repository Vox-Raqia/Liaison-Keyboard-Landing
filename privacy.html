Privacy

How Liaison Keyboard handles message context, consent, and billing data.

This policy covers both liaisonkeyboard.com and app.liaisonkeyboard.com and is written to match the actual product: assistive drafting, explicit opt-ins, and controlled continuity rather than hidden automation.

Last updated

March 30, 2026.

Operating model

Generated replies are assistive output that you review and copy yourself.

Key principle

Optional continuity and memory features require explicit user choice.

Collection and processing

What Liaison Keyboard processes

Liaison Keyboard collects account details, billing metadata, submitted message context entered during generation sessions, and product telemetry necessary to operate and improve the service. Nothing is sent on your behalf from the marketing site.

Analytics

Measurement and traffic signals

This site and the app use Google Analytics 4 with measurement ID G-FMVPQNPPDD to collect aggregated page-view, session, referral, and device-type information.

Google handles that analytics processing under Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out across sites with the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies and storage

Small, named storage only

  • liaison_cookie_consent stores your saved cookie preference for up to 180 days.
  • lk_deeplink stores referral and scenario handoff context only if continuity cookies are enabled.
  • liaison_auth_hint stores a lightweight session hint for navigation continuity only if continuity cookies are enabled.

All optional continuity cookies are gated behind your explicit choice in the banner or on the cookie settings page.

Continuity

Attribution and referral tracking

When you arrive with parameters such as utm_source, ref_id, or scenario_id, Liaison Keyboard may store those values locally so they can be passed into the app during the same visit or a return visit. This is used for continuity and internal analytics only.

Authentication and storage

Managed account access and user-scoped storage

Account authentication and user-scoped data storage for the app experience are handled through a managed backend service. User-submitted content is stored in isolated, user-scoped containers and is not used to train or improve AI models without explicit opt-in.

Interactive site features

Client-side marketing interactions only

The FAQ accordion and the hero example switcher on the marketing site are fully client-side features. They do not transmit data to any server and do not set cookies or modify browser storage.

Memory

Full Memory is opt-in

Full Memory is a privacy-first opt-in feature available on Pro plans. It is not active by default and requires explicit consent inside the app.

Isolation

Thread isolation stays explicit

Memory containers are designed to prevent one user's conversation context from leaking into another, and thread linking remains a user-directed action rather than a default behavior.

Retention

How long data and signals remain

Attribution cookies and session hints expire on the schedules noted above. Google Analytics data is retained according to the configured retention setting in the associated property. Account and message data in the app is retained while your account is active.

Your choices

Controls remain explicit and available

You can manage cookie preferences from the cookie settings page, clear local storage through your browser, disable Full Memory inside the app, and request account deletion or a copy of your data through support.

Vendors and contact

Billing and privacy support

  • Payment processing, subscription management, invoices, and the customer billing portal are handled by Stripe.
  • Liaison Keyboard does not store raw payment card data.
  • Stripe's handling of payment information is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Privacy questions, data requests, or policy concerns can be sent to support@liaisonkeyboard.com.
