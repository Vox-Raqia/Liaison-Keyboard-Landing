## The Story (First Comment)

**The problem I kept running into**

I'd get a text that made my stomach drop — a last-minute boss demand, mixed signals from someone I'm dating, a guilt-tripping family member. My instinct was either to over-apologize, get defensive, or go silent. Every time, I'd hit send and immediately wonder: "Why did I say it that way?"

**What most advice gets wrong**

Most communication advice is one-size-fits-all. "Be confident." "Be polite." But real conversations are nuanced. The same "firm but fair" reply that works with a boss can feel cold with a partner. I needed something that understood context, tone, and power dynamics — not just generic scripts.

**The solution I built**

Liaison Reply sits between your brain and the send button.

You paste in the message + any off-screen context (what's really going on, what you're trying to achieve). The AI analyzes subtext, emotional charge, and social dynamics — then surfaces three reply paths:
- **Natural** — how you'd respond if you weren't anxious
- **Keep it going** — moves the conversation forward constructively
- **Short** — clean boundary, no drama

You stay in control. You edit, you copy, you send. The tool never auto-sends. It's not a chatbot writing for you — it's a mirror that shows you your best options so you can choose authentically.

**Why this matters**

Because the gap between receiving a message and forming a reply is where we lose our clarity. Anxiety hijacks that space. Liaison Reply reclaims it. You get to respond — not react.

We're in pre-launch, free to use. If you've ever sent a text you regretted, give it a try.

## FAQ (Anticipated)

**Q: Does this auto-send my replies?**  
A: No. You always copy or Edit & Copy. You send manually. We're human-in-the-loop only.

**Q: Is this another chatbot wrapper?**  
A: No. We don't chat. We give you three discrete, polished reply paths that you control.

**Q: How is this different from Grammarly or ChatGPT?**  
A: Grammarly fixes grammar. ChatGPT writes for you (and often loses your voice). Liaison Reply analyzes social dynamics and gives you grounded options that preserve your intent — you stay the author.

**Q: Is it free?**  
A: Yes, free tier available. Pro tier adds Full Memory (persistent context across conversations) and Premium Personas. No paywall to try the core three-path engine.

**Q: Do you sell my data?**  
A: No. Privacy-respecting design. Your messages stay in your control. We don't train on user data.
