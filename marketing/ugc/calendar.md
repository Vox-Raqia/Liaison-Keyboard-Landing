# UGC Content Calendar — Wave 1 (First 3 Pieces)

## Phase 1: Foundation (Pre-Launch)

### Piece 1 — "Before/After Text Split-Screen" (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- **Platform**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- **Scheduled**: Launch Day (P0)
- **Format**: 60-second vertical video, split-screen
- **Hook**: "Ever sent a text you immediately regretted? Here's what I used to do vs. what I do now."
- **Structure**:
  - 0–10s: Problem — showing a high-stakes message on screen, anxious reaction
  - 10–30s: Old behavior (apologize, over-explain, escalate)
  - 30–50s: New behavior (use Liaison Reply, three options shown)
  - 50–60s: CTA — "Link in bio, free to start"
- **Assets**: Screen recordings of the app, voice-over or captions
- **Hashtag set**: #communication #emotionalintelligence #texting #boundaries #datingadvice #workplace

### Piece 2 — "Three Reply Paths Explained" (Carousel)
- **Platform**: Instagram, LinkedIn
- **Scheduled**: Launch Day + 2 days
- **Format**: 5-slide carousel
- **Hook**: "Most reply advice is one-size-fits-all. That's wrong."
- **Slides**:
  1. Problem: generic advice doesn't fit nuanced situations
  2. Natural reply — when to use it
  3. Keep it going — when to use it
  4. Short — when to use it
  5. CTA: "Get your three options at LiaisonReply.com"
- **Assets**: Styled screenshots from `press/screenshots/`
- **Hashtag set**: #communicationtips #replystrategy #emotionalintelligence #selfadvocacy

### Piece 3 — "Workplace Boundary Setting" (Twitter/X Thread + LinkedIn post)
- **Platform**: Twitter/X, LinkedIn
- **Scheduled**: Launch Day + 5 days
- **Format**: Thread (5 tweets) / single-post variant for LinkedIn
- **Hook**: "Your boss sends a last-minute Friday night request. Here's how to say 'no' without saying 'no'."
- **Content**: Real(ish) scenario + one of the three generated replies + explanation of why it works
- **CTA**: "Try it free: LiaisonReply.com"
- **Hashtag set**: #workplaceboundaries #professionalcommunication #careeradvice #management

## Notes

- All content is **zero-dollar production** — screen recordings, static images, text overlays only
- Cross-post adapt: same core asset repurposed per platform constraints
- Tagging strategy: avoid direct competitor mentions; focus on problem spaces (text anxiety, boundary-setting, communication clarity)
- UGC tone: authentic, slightly rough edges OK — not slick ad content
