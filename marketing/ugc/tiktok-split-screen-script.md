# TikTok Split-Screen Script (60 seconds)

**Format:** Vertical video, left side = "before Liaison", right side = "after Liaison"

---

**[0–10s] Problem Hook (both sides show same phone screen)**

> (Voiceover or on-screen text:)
> "You open your phone. It's that text. The one that makes your stomach drop."

**Visual:**
- Left: anxious person (you) looking at phone, biting lip, pacing
- Right: same person taking a breath, calm
- On-screen message (centered): "I need that deck cleaned up before noon. The last version missed the point and I do not want another round of confusion."

---

**[10–30s] Old Behavior (left side only)**

> "Old me would've either... over-apologized, or gotten defensive, or just done it silently resentfully."

**Visual (left side only, right side dark/blank):**
- Quick cuts of you typing: "So sorry, I'll fix it!!" (apologize)
- Quick cut: "Actually that's not fair..." (defensive)
- Quick cut: You throwing phone on bed, frustrated (silent resentment)
- Show resulting conversation thread escalating or going cold

---

**[30–50s] New Behavior (right side only)**

> "Now I pause. I paste the message into Liaison Reply. It gives me three options — Natural, Proactive, Short — each one calibrated to hold my boundary without burning the bridge."

**Visual (right side active, left side dark):**
- Screen recording of Liaison Reply interface
- Message appears in app input
- Three reply cards animate in: Natural, Proactive, Short
- You tap one, edit slightly, copy, paste back to text
- You send: "Understood. I'll get this finished tonight. Going forward, I need earlier notice to maintain quality."
- Show reply received: "Thanks. Appreciate it."

---

**[50–60s] Outcome & CTA**

> "Same message. Different outcome. I kept my composure, set a clearer standard, and actually improved the working relationship. It's not magic — it's just having your options visible. Try it free at the link."

**Visual:**
- Split shows both sides side-by-side contrasting
- Final screen: Liaison Reply logo + tagline "Slow down. See your options. Respond with clarity."
- URL overlay: LiaisonReply.com

---

**Production Notes:**
- Use phone screen recorder for app footage
- Can be done with just a phone and basic video editor (CapCut, InShot)
- Captions mandatory (most users watch muted)
- No need for professional lighting or voice — text-to-speech is acceptable
- Music: lo-fi, calm instrumental (YouTube Audio Library / TikTok commercial sounds)
