Cookie and persistence policy for Liaison Keyboard.

Last updated: March 25, 2026. This policy covers both `liaisonkeyboard.com` and `app.liaisonkeyboard.com`.

Cookie settings

Necessary storage stays on for core site functionality. Optional continuity cookies only control whether Liaison Keyboard remembers attribution and scenario handoff data across visits.

Attribution and continuity

Liaison Keyboard may store lightweight referral or scenario identifiers so a user who starts on the landing site can continue into the app without losing context. Examples include `utm_source`, `ref_id`, and `scenario_id`.

Privacy-first opt-in Full Memory

Full Memory is not treated as passive surveillance. It is framed and marketed as a privacy-first opt-in feature. When enabled, it allows Liaison Keyboard to maintain persistent conversational context inside isolated memory containers under user control.

Session awareness

The landing site may use a root-domain cookie or local browser storage to decide whether to show `Start Free Generation` or `Back to Dashboard`. This is limited to navigation continuity and does not expose conversation content on the marketing site.

How to revoke consent

Users can clear site storage in their browser and disable memory features inside the app experience. Support requests can be sent to support@liaisonkeyboard.com.
