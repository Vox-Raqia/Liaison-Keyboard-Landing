## Key Features

- **Three-path reply system** — Natural (standard), Keep it going (proactive), Short (concise)
- **Emotional de-escalation support** — Grounds charged messages without suppressing your voice
- **Tone and clarity refinement** — Polish your intent into language that lands
- **Privacy-respecting design** — Your messages stay in your control; no data selling

## Target Audience

- Professionals managing difficult workplace conversations
- Individuals navigating complex dating and relationship ambiguity
- Anyone setting boundaries with family or friends
- People who pause before hitting send on high-stakes texts
