FAQ

Questions before you start.

Clear answers about how it works, what you pay for, and what stays in your control.

What exactly is Liaison Keyboard?

Liaison Keyboard is a web app that helps you answer difficult texts. You paste the message, review three reply options, edit the one that fits, and send it yourself.

Does anything auto-send?

No. Liaison Keyboard never sends on your behalf. You review, edit, copy, and decide what goes back into the conversation.

What do I get with the free tier?

You get 15 free generations so you can test the full workflow before paying. That is enough to see whether the product helps you think and reply more clearly.

What changes when I upgrade to Pro?

Pro gives you more room to keep going when the conversation gets difficult, plus deeper memory and premium personas for higher-stakes situations.

Paid plans still include fair-use and misuse protections.

How is billing handled?

Billing and subscription management run through Stripe. You can manage billing, change intervals, and cancel from the customer portal.

Can I cancel or switch plans later?

Yes. You can manage your subscription through Stripe at any time, including cancellation and billing interval changes.

What happens to my messages and data?

Right now, Liaison Keyboard runs on the web. Nothing auto-sends, and only the content you choose to save stays in your account.

Why is it called Liaison Keyboard?

We call it Liaison Keyboard because the product is built around the moment you are deciding what to type next, not after the conversation has already gone sideways. Today that workflow lives on the web. Longer term, we are working toward Android and iPhone keyboard integrations so Liaison Keyboard can sit closer to the real conversation while you still stay in control of what gets sent.

Who is Liaison Keyboard best for?

People who need help answering loaded messages at work, in dating, or with family without freezing, spiraling, or firing off something they regret.
