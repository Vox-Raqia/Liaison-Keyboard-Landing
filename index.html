FAQ

Questions before you commit.

We believe in transparency. Here's what you need to know before getting started.

What exactly is Liaison?

Liaison is a Communication Prosthetic—a tactical intelligence layer between your brain and the send button. It analyzes incoming messages, understands the social dynamics, and generates calibrated response options so you can communicate with confidence.

Does anything auto-send?

Never. You review every generated response before copying. You can edit it, discard it, or use it as-is. Only what you explicitly save is stored.

What do I get with the free tier?

15 generations to test the full workflow. That's enough to understand how Liaison works and decide if it fits your communication style before committing.

What's in the Pro upgrade?

Unlimited generations, all premium personas, full conversation memory across threads, and priority support. It's designed for those who rely on strategic communication daily.

How is billing handled?

Checkout and subscription management via Stripe portal. Installing billing, invoices, receipts, and subscription management.

Can I cancel Pro later?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription through the Stripe Customer Portal at any time.

Do I need full keyboard access?

No. You run the current web app without full keyboard access.
