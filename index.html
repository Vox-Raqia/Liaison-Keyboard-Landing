What does Liaison do?

It helps you paste a message, choose a tone, and generate three reply options you review yourself.

Does anything send automatically?

No. Liaison drafts replies for you to review and copy back yourself.

Checkout and subscription management go through Stripe, including invoices, payment methods, and cancellations.

Can I cancel Pro later?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription through the Stripe customer portal.

Do I need full keyboard access?

No. You can try the current web app without granting full keyboard access.
